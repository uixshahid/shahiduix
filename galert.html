UI/UX DESIGN – G ALERT

An app for quick disaster alerts and instant emergency response.

Year

2024

Client

G-ALERT

Role

UI/UX DESIGNER

Project

G-ALERT MOBILE APP

Description

G-Alert is a mobile UI/UX project built to empower users during emergencies and natural disasters. The app focuses on delivering rapid alerts, intuitive SOS activation, and reliable location tracking through a clean, minimal interface optimized for high-stress use. It supports individuals and communities by offering real-time updates, emergency contact tools, and clear, accessible navigation to ensure fast action during critical situations.

Inspired by user-centered design principles for emergency tech, the goal was to create a system that feels immediate, secure, and purpose-driven—balancing visual simplicity with functional depth. From alert screens to accessible UI components, every element is crafted to minimize user friction and maximize safety, resulting in a responsive, trustworthy, and life-saving experience tailored for crisis environments.
