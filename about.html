I’m Shahid, a UI/UX designer.

I’m a Calicut-based digital designer with a strong foundation in graphic design and a growing focus on UI/UX, web design, and visual development. My creative journey began with static visuals, but over time, I’ve gravitated toward crafting meaningful, interactive digital experiences.

This transition from graphic design to product design was intentional—driven by curiosity and a desire to improve usability and interaction. I now focus on building responsive layouts and intuitive user flows that prioritize clarity and function.

I design with tools like Figma, Illustrator, and Photoshop, blending creativity with structure. My approach combines visual design and UX thinking to create purposeful digital experiences.
